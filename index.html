About Us

At Alyassin Marine Services, we specialize in providing efficient and eco-friendly garbage collection services for the maritime industry. Our mission is to ensure clean and sustainable operations by managing waste responsibly, adhering to international environmental standards.

Our Services

• Waste Collection & Disposal: Safe and efficient removal of waste materials from ships and marine vessels.

• Recycling Solutions: Promoting sustainability by sorting and processing recyclable materials.

• Customized Solutions: Tailored waste management plans to meet the specific needs of our clients. With a dedicated team and modern equipment, we are committed to maintaining the cleanliness and safety of marine environments. Choose us for reliable, timely, and environmentally conscious waste management solutions.
